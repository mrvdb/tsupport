Secret chats are the most secure type of communication in
Telegram. All messages and media in Secret Chats are encrypted using
end-to-end encryption, which means only you and your chat partner can
access them. You can also set a self-destruct timer in Secret Chats —
once the timer runs out, the messages disappear from both devices.

In the latest version of official Telegram apps, when a message is
deleted from a Secret Chat on your device (even without the timer) it
is also deleted from your partner's device.

You can find more info on Secret Chats and self-destruct timers in the FAQ:
  https://telegram.org/faq#secret-chats
